Random arXiv Paper

Select a field and category, then click "Get Random Paper" to display a random arXiv paper.

This tool helps researchers and curious minds discover new academic papers from arXiv.org by randomly selecting publications based on your field of interest. It's designed to promote serendipitous discovery and broaden your academic horizons. The articles are selected from the 2000 most recent entries in the category.
